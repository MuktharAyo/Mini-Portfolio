Steps to Prepare Popcorn

  1. Grab a bag of popcorn kernels.
  2. Heat a pan with a little oil on medium heat.
  3. Add a few kernels to the pan and wait for them to pop.
  4. Once they pop, add the rest of the kernels in an even layer.
  5. Cover the pan and shake it occasionally until the popping slows down.
  6. Remove from heat, pour into a bowl, and season with salt or butter.
